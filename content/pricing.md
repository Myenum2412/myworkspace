### Frequently Asked Questions (Pricing)

**Why don't you charge per user?**
Because per-seat pricing punishes growth. Every time you hire, your costs go up — and that creates friction between growing your team and managing your budget. We believe you should pay for the platform and get unlimited use of it, whether you have 10 employees or 500. MyWorkSpace is a fixed cost that scales with your business, not against it.

**What does "self-hosted" mean for pricing?**
You deploy MyWorkSpace on your own server (or cloud instance). You pay a one-time platform fee (or choose the free tier). There are no recurring per-user subscription fees. Your only ongoing costs are your own infrastructure — server, storage, bandwidth — which you were already paying for anyway.

**Is the Free tier really free?**
Yes. No time limit. No credit card required. No feature limitations that expire after 30 days. 10 users, 5 GB storage, core features. We built it because small teams deserve professional tools without budget barriers.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
All major credit and debit cards, processed securely through Stripe. Enterprise tier also supports invoicing and purchase orders.

**Can I upgrade from Free to Growth?**
Yes. Upgrade at any time from your billing settings. Your data, projects, files, and configuration carry over seamlessly. No migration, no disruption.

**Can I downgrade if I don't need Growth anymore?**
Yes. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Your data remains intact — you just lose access to Growth-tier features until you upgrade again.

**Do you offer refunds?**
Yes. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid tiers with no questions asked. We're confident MyWorkSpace will deliver value. But if it doesn't work for you, we'd rather refund you than have you stay unhappy.

**What if I need a custom plan?**
Contact our sales team. We regularly customize deployments, pricing, and support SLAs for enterprise clients. We can also accommodate non-standard deployment requirements, custom feature development, and professional services engagements.

**Is there a discount for non-profits or educational institutions?**
Yes. We offer discounted pricing for qualified non-profit organizations and accredited educational institutions. Contact our sales team with your details.

**How does the 14-day guarantee work?**
Purchase any paid tier. Use the platform for up to 14 days. If you decide it's not the right fit, contact support and we'll process a full refund — no questions asked, no paperwork, no hassle.

**What happens if I exceed my storage limit?**
Free tier: 5 GB. Growth tier: 50 GB. Enterprise tier: custom. If you approach your limit, you'll receive in-app notifications. Growth and Enterprise tiers can purchase additional storage in 10 GB blocks.

**Do I need to provide a credit card for the Free tier?**
No. No credit card required. Sign up, deploy, and use the platform. We only ask for payment information when you choose to upgrade.
