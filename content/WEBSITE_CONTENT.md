## HOMEPAGE — HOW IT WORKS

**Section Headline:** From Zero to Fully Operational in Under an Hour.

**Step 1: Deploy on Your Infrastructure**
Get your own server (AWS, DigitalOcean, on-prem — anywhere). Run the deployment script. Docker Compose or PM2 — your choice. Caddy handles HTTPS automatically. **Time: ~15 minutes.**

**Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace**
Configure your organization profile. Invite your team. Assign roles (Super Admin to Staff — 9 levels). Structure departments and teams. **Time: ~20 minutes.**

**Step 3: Start Working**
Create your first project. Break it into tasks on the kanban board. Upload files to organized folders. Track time against work. Real-time notifications keep everyone in sync. **This is where the magic happens.**

**Step 4: Invite Clients**
Give clients their own login. They see their projects, their files, their dashboards — and nothing else. Granular permissions mean you control exactly what's visible. **Clients feel informed. You look professional.**

**Step 5: Invoice and Analyze**
Time tracked against projects flows directly into billing data. Generate invoices. Review profitability reports. See revenue trends. All without exporting a single CSV. **Data-driven decisions, not gut feels.**

## HOMEPAGE — FAQs

**What is MyWorkSpace?**
A self-hosted, all-in-one workforce management and team collaboration platform combining employee management, project tracking, file management, time tracking, client portals, billing, and analytics into one unified system.

**Who is it for?**
Organizations from 5-person startups to 500+ person enterprises across IT services, construction, marketing, consulting, manufacturing, real estate, healthcare, and more.

**How is it different from Asana, Google Drive, or Toggl?**
Those are point solutions that each solve one problem. MyWorkSpace replaces 5–7 of them in one integrated platform. Your data stays in one place. Your team works in one place. Your reporting comes from one source of truth.

**Can I self-host?**
Absolutely. MyWorkSpace is designed for self-hosted deployment on any Linux server using Docker Compose or PM2. All deployment scripts and configuration templates are included. Your data never touches third-party infrastructure.

**Is there an API?**
Yes. A full REST API covers all modules — auth, users, tasks, projects, files, time entries, organizations, clients, billing, search, and more.

**How secure is my data?**
bcrypt password hashing, JWE-encrypted session tokens, TOTP-based 2FA, CSRF protection, rate limiting, input sanitization, comprehensive audit logging, ClamAV virus scanning — all standard.

**What if I need help deploying it?**
Free tier includes documentation and community support. Growth tier includes deployment guides. Enterprise tier includes white-glove deployment assistance with a dedicated engineer.

**Can I migrate from another tool?**
Yes. Most of our customers migrate from a combination of Asana/Jira, Google Drive/Dropbox, Toggl/Harvest, and BambooHR. We provide migration guides and support for data transfer.
