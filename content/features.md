# MyWorkSpace — Features Page

**Page Title:** Features — Everything Your Business Needs in One Platform

**Meta Description:** Explore every feature of MyWorkSpace — workforce management, project tracking, enterprise file management, time tracking, client portals, billing, analytics, and real-time collaboration. All self-hosted.

**H1 Headline:** The Only Platform Your Team Will Ever Need

**Subheadline:** Twenty integrated modules. One self-hosted deployment. Zero tool-switching. Your entire operation — people, projects, files, time, clients, and finances — in one place.

**Intro Paragraph:**
Most businesses use 5–10 separate tools to manage their operations. Each one does one thing well, but together they create chaos — data lives in silos, team members juggle logins, and answers to simple questions like "is this project profitable?" require exporting and reconciling data from multiple systems. MyWorkSpace eliminates that chaos by bringing every function into one unified platform. Here's what that looks like.

### Module 1: Workforce & Employee Management

**The Pain:**
You have employee records scattered across spreadsheets, email attachments, and a half-configured HR system. Onboarding a new hire means creating accounts in 5 different tools. Offboarding is the same nightmare in reverse. You don't have a single view of who's in your organization, what they do, or what's in their file.

**What It Does:**
Centralized employee lifecycle management from offer letter to offboarding. Rich employee profiles, department and team structuring, document management, and a self-service staff portal that empowers every team member.

**Subfeatures:**

- **Employee Directory** — Searchable, filterable directory with rich profiles including personal info, experience, education, dependents, and documents
- **Department Management** — Structure your organization with departments, sub-departments, and team leads. Drag-and-drop reorganizations
- **Team Management** — Create cross-functional teams with leads and member roles for project-based collaboration
- **Document Repository** — Store resumes, offer letters, certificates, performance reviews, and compliance docs alongside employee profiles. Versioned. Searchable. Always accessible.
- **Self-Service Portal** — Employees manage their own tasks, timesheets, attendance, files, and performance goals. Reduces HR administrative burden by 40%+
- **Terminated Employees** — Gracefully handle offboarding with alumni tracking and data preservation

**Why It Matters:**
Eliminate the paper chase. Every employee record, document, and performance metric lives in one searchable place. HR spends less time filing and more time supporting people. New hires go from offer to productive in hours, not weeks.

**Who Loves It:** HR Managers, Operations Directors, Team Leads

**Subfeatures:**

- **Progress Monitoring** — Percentage completion, milestone tracking, and auto-generated status reports
- **Client Linking** — Associate projects with clients so they appear automatically in the client portal

**Why It Matters:**
Project managers get a single pane of glass for every active engagement. No more chasing status updates across email threads. Budget alerts arrive before you overspend, not after.

**Who Loves It:** Project Managers, Account Managers, Operations Directors

### Module 4: Enterprise File Management

**The Pain:**
Your files are everywhere — Google Drive, Dropbox, email attachments, local hard drives, USB sticks. Nobody knows which version is current. "final_v3_reviewed_FINAL(2).pdf" is a real filename in your organization. Clients request files and you spend 15 minutes finding the right one.

**What It Does:** A full-featured file system with folder hierarchy, resumable uploads up to 10 GB, versioning with rollback, secure sharing, and approval workflows. Your files, under control.

**Subfeatures:**

- **Folder Tree Navigation** — Intuitive folder hierarchy with drag-and-drop organization. Familiar. Fast. Works like you expect.
- **Resumable Uploads (TUS Protocol)** — Upload files up to 10 GB that survive network interruptions. No more watching a 45-minute upload fail at 98%.
- **File Versioning** — Every modification creates a numbered version. Roll back to any prior version with one click. Version comments explain what changed and why.
- **File Locking** — Lock files during active editing to prevent conflicting changes. No more two people editing the same file and overwriting each other.
- **Secure External Sharing** — Password-protected links with expiration dates and download limits. Share with clients confidently.
- **Internal Sharing** — Share files between team members with granular view/edit permissions
- **Recycle Bin** — Soft-delete with full restore; permanent delete only on explicit action. Accidents are reversible.
- **Bulk Operations** — Delete, restore, move, tag 50 files at once. Not one by one.
- **Duplicate Detection** — Automatic detection when someone uploads a file that already exists. Saves storage. Prevents confusion.
- **Approval Workflow** — Route files through approval steps before publishing. Perfect for legal review, compliance sign-off, or creative approval.
- **Virus Scanning** — ClamAV integration scans every upload for threats. Security doesn't slow you down.
- **File Categories** — Organize as General, Profile, or Report with category-based access controls

**Why It Matters:**
Your files are your business. Version control means no more filename chaos. Secure sharing means you control exactly who sees what. The 10 GB upload limit means even your largest CAD files, videos, and design files transfer reliably.

**Who Loves It:** Project Managers, Creative Teams, Engineers, Legal & Compliance

### Module 5: Time Tracking & Attendance

**The Pain:**
You track time in one tool, run payroll in another, and bill clients based on a third. Reconciling them is a monthly nightmare. You suspect you're leaking billable hours. Attendance is tracked on paper or not at all.

**What It Does:** Session-based and manual time tracking with break management, billable hour tracking, and attendance reporting. What gets tracked gets managed.

**Subfeatures:**

- **Session-Based Tracking** — Clock in/out with automatic session recording. One click start. One click stop.
- **Manual Entry** — Log time retroactively with project and task association. Perfect for when you forget to start the timer.
- **Break Management** — Automatic break detection and manual break logging. Pay accurate. Don't overpay.
- **Billable vs. Non-Billable** — Flag hours as billable per project and client. See exactly how much revenue each project is generating.
- **Team Time Summaries** — Manager-level view of who's billable, who's overloaded, who has capacity
- **Time Reports** — Aggregated reports by project, employee, client, and date range. Export or review in-platform.
- **Attendance Dashboard** — Real-time view of who's online, on break, or offline. Know who's working without asking.
- **Attendance Reports** — Exportable attendance summaries for payroll. Connect to your payroll provider.

**Why It Matters:**
Capture every billable minute. Stop leaving money on the table. Understand utilization across your team — who's at 80% capacity and who's drowning. Feed accurate data directly into client invoices and payroll. No more end-of-month time sheet hunts.

**Who Loves It:** Finance Managers, Project Managers, Agency Owners, Operations Directors

### Module 10: Security & Administration

**The Pain:**
You have sensitive employee and client data spread across half a dozen platforms, each with its own security model. You're not sure who has access to what. If you were audited tomorrow, you'd struggle to produce a clean access log.

**What It Does:** Enterprise-grade security including role-based access control, multi-factor authentication, audit logging, and comprehensive administration tools.

**Subfeatures:**

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** — 9 hierarchical roles with granular Casbin permissions per entity type
- **Multi-Factor Authentication** — TOTP-based 2FA with setup, verification, and challenge flows. Works with any authenticator app.
- **OAuth / Social Login** — Google, GitHub, and LinkedIn OAuth providers for frictionless onboarding
- **Account Lockout** — Automatic 15-minute lockout after 5 failed login attempts. Brute force protection baked in.
- **Session Management** — Online/break/offline tracking with active session monitoring. See all active sessions. Revoke any session.
- **CSRF Protection** — Custom middleware preventing cross-site request forgery attacks
- **Rate Limiting** — Tiered limits per endpoint: auth (10/min), upload (30/min), search (30/min), API (100/min), share download (20/min)
- **Input Sanitization** — Automatic sanitization of all user inputs to prevent injection attacks
- **Audit Logging** — Comprehensive, exportable audit trail with request correlation IDs. Every action. Every user. Every timestamp.
- **Admin Panel** — System-wide statistics, user management, organization listing, permission viewer, system logs
- **Request Correlation IDs** — Every request tagged with a unique ID for debugging and tracing
- **OpenTelemetry + Prometheus** — Metrics exported for monitoring with Grafana dashboards
- **Sentry Error Tracking** — Real-time error monitoring and alerting. We know about problems before you do.

**Why It Matters:**
Security isn't a feature — it's a foundation. MyWorkSpace gives you the controls you need to protect your data, pass audits, and sleep well at night. RBAC means sensitive HR data is visible only to HR. 2FA means compromised passwords aren't enough. Audit trails mean you have the receipts.

**Who Loves It:** IT Managers, CTOs, Compliance Officers, Security-Conscious Business Owners
